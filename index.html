What drives us

"Old cars aren't dead, they are the dad of all cars"

We love classic cars and want to share our passion with others. In 2016 Henry Maiava founded Classic Garage with two cars and a dream. Few years later Classic Garage has a fleet of six beautiful classic cars and 8 employees. We believe that old cars aren't dead, but rather they are the dad of all cars. Our purpose is to make people understand the heritage of cars and appreciate the work and meaning that has invested into this important part of our history. It doesn't matter if you need a classic car for your wedding, an eyecatcher for your graduation entrance, our you just want to treat yourself with a little something for the weekend; we got you!
